Born as Snowflake Creative in 2005, we were amongst the first generation of digital-first agencies. Since then we've designed and executed hundreds of websites, brands and marketing campaigns for businesses around the globe. Our now owner and managing director Simon was fresh out of college then, but it didn't take him long to get hooked into digital marketing, design, and dare we say, development (we don't let him touch code these days though!).
In 2013 we opened our Katowice studio after a long freelance relationship with Bartek, our head of technology. Since then we've grown an unrivaled in-house team of technical experts, creative designers and clever marketers.
In 2015 it was time we grew up and changed our trading name to something that reflects our agency. Bigger Picture is more than a name to us. It reflects our mission to always question the norm, see more than what is in front of us, and truly embrace every project we work on.
